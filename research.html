Research Activity

My research focuses on AI/ML techniques, particularly in autonomous systems, image processing, and decentralized coordination in wireless networks. I am passionate about using advanced technologies to solve real-world problems, especially in areas like autism detection, Alzheimer's disease prediction, and urban infrastructure growth prediction using satellite imagery.

I am an active member of the IEEE Student, IEEE Standards Association, and other academic groups contributing to AI and machine learning research.

Publications

"Unlocking Deva Nagri: OCR with CNN"

Saket Maheshwari, Dr. Saynatan Sinha. IEEE World Conference on Applied Intelligence and Computing (AIC 2024).

This paper presents a novel method for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for Devanagari script using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). We discuss the design, methodology, and evaluation of the system's performance, and its potential applications in various sectors such as education and document digitization.

"Automated Detection Of Autism Spectrum Disorder In Children Via Standardized Assessment-Based Deep Learning Models"

Saket Maheshwari,arXiv, 2024.

This paper explores the application of deep learning models to automatically detect Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) in children based on standardized assessments. We present the methodology, model performance, and implications for early intervention and diagnostics in healthcare.

Download


"Advancing Alzheimer's Disease Prediction Through Image Processing: A GFCC and Neural Network Approach"

Saket Maheshwari, Mr. Rahul Kumar Submitted for publication, 2024.

This research paper focuses on enhancing Alzheimer's disease prediction using image processing techniques such as GFCC (Gray-Level Co-occurrence Matrix) and neural networks. The study evaluates the accuracy of these techniques in classifying Alzheimer's cases from medical imaging data.

Paper under review.


"Refined Horizons: A Comparative Study of Optimized Image Classification Through Transfer Learning"

Saket Maheshwari.COCO Dataset, 2024.

In this comparative study, we explore the use of transfer learning techniques in optimizing image classification models. We test multiple architectures and highlight the trade-offs between performance and computational efficiency, with applications in object detection and satellite image analysis.

"Bridging Ancient Wisdom and Modern Tech: AI-Enhanced Smart Yoga Mat with Multimodal Sensing"

Saket Maheshwari. Research Paper, 2024.

This research integrates AI technologies with smart yoga mats to provide real-time tracking and feedback on posture, balance, and form. It explores multimodal sensing techniques for improved precision and personalized yoga experiences.
